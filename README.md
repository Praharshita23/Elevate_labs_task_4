# Adventure Works Sales Performance Dashboard – Power BI

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to analyze business sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and regional profitability. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## Objective

* Analyze overall sales, profit, and return performance
* Identify high-performing products, customers, and regions
* Understand customer demographics and purchasing patterns
* Provide actionable insights to support strategic decision-making

---

## Dataset

The dashboard uses the **Adventure Works Sales Dataset**, which includes information on:

* Sales transactions and returns
* Customer demographics and purchasing behavior
* Product categories and product models
* Geographic sales regions and country-level performance
* Order dates for time-series analysis

---

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard consists of multiple interactive analytical pages:

* **KPI Overview:** Total revenue, profit, orders, quantity sold, and return rate
* **Sales & Returns Performance:** Revenue trends over time and return analysis
* **Customer Performance:** Customer distribution by occupation, income, age group, and gender
* **Product Performance:** Top-performing product categories, models, and subcategories
* **Geographical Performance:** Revenue and profit distribution across regions and countries
* **Insights & Recommendations:** Business insights derived from dashboard analysis

---

## Key Insights

* Customers aged **45–65** generate the highest total revenue
* **Road Bikes and Mountain Bikes** dominate overall product sales
* **North America** contributes the highest revenue and profit
* Working professionals form the largest customer purchasing segment
